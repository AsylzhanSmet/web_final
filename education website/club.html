Book Club

Book Club is an intellectual and cultural space that unites students in exciting and educational activities. This club aims not only to encourage reading, but also to create an atmosphere that encourages the exchange of ideas, discussion and self-development. Every month we gather to discuss a selected book, where participants have the opportunity to express their impressions, analyze the plot, discuss the themes and motifs of the work, and share their thoughts and views.

Tengri – Debate club

If you like to discuss and prove logical chains, learn to see cause-and-effect relationships, and have good soft-skills, this club is for you. • Domestic policy: meetings and events every week, lectures, trainings from good debaters, internal tournaments • Foreign policy: active interaction with other Astana clubs, joint meetings, tournaments, agreements • Active student life and environment, high SSCI, organizational composition of events, coordinators, mentors.

Board Games Club

Board games club - join us in the world of strategic entertainment! Students gather to play a variety of board games, including traditional board games, card games, and role-playing games (e.g. DND, etc.). Inclusion is key at our club - everyone is welcome to come and join in the fun! Take part in friendly competitions, make new friends and explore the exciting world of board games with us!

Google Developer Student Club (GDSC)

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a university community for students interested in Google developer technologies. It was created with the aim of bringing together students from all undergraduate and graduate programs who are looking to develop their skills in the field of development. By joining GDSC, students have the opportunity to expand their knowledge by working in a peer-to-peer learning environment and interact with experienced developers.
